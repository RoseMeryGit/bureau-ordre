Go To _variables.scss (assets/scss/_variables.scss )

How to Change Logo ?

Go To "assets/images/brand" folder and replace your logo with Previous Logos within in image size. Note: Please don't increase logo sizes. Replace your logo within given image size. otherwise the logo will not fit in particular place it disturbs the template design.

LTR Version to RTL Version

How to Enable RTL version?

open custom-switcher.js path:(Dashlead/assets/js/custom-switcher.js)file and remove comments for html.setAttribute("dir" , "rtl") to enable RTL version.

Light Theme & Dark Theme

How to Enable Light Theme?

open custom-switcher.js path:(Dashlead/assets/js/custom-switcher.js)file and remove comments for html.setAttribute("data-theme-color" , "light") to enable Light Theme.

How to Enable Dark Theme?

open custom-switcher.js path:(Dashlead/assets/js/custom-switcher.js)file and remove comments for html.setAttribute("data-theme-color" , "dark") to enable Dark Theme.
